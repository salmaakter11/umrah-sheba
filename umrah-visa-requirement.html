How can someone from Bangladesh apply for a visa to Umrah?

The process of applying for an Umrah visa from Bangladesh is simple, but it does need close attention to detail. Here is a step-by-step tutorial to assist you with the application process:

  • Select a trustworthy Hajj and Umrah company.
  • Fill out the application for a visa.
  • Prepare the necessary documents
  • Await the blessing
  • Plans for travel
  • Do the Umrah.
  • assemble the necessary paperwork (passport, immunisation record, etc.)
  • Get a letter of consent from the mahram (For women)
  • Send in your application
  • Wait for Approval
  • Fee payment

Frequently asked questions

To apply online, visit the official Saudi Arabian embassy or consulate website, fill out the visa application form accurately, and upload the required documents.

The process for obtaining an Umrah visa in 2023 remains similar to previous years. Eligible Bangladeshi pilgrims can apply through authorized agencies or directly through the Saudi Arabian embassy.

The Umrah visa processing time from Bangladesh may vary, but it typically takes a few days to a few weeks. Applying well enough ahead of your chosen travel dates is advised.

The Umrah visa fee may vary depending on the type of Umrah visa (individual, family, group) and other factors. The fee is subject to change, so confirming the exact amount with your chosen agency or embassy is essential.
