Package itinerary

Begin the Umrah.

Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport (Terminal 2/3)
  • Show the passport and enter the airport
  • When the checking is done, give the baggage on the belt
  • Get the boarding pass from the designated booth of the airline
  • Complete the immigration
  • Wearing Ihram clothes (It is to be worn in mikah or before mikah)
  • Get on the plane when it's time
Depart to Madinah Airport

Begin your journey from DAC to MED

Landed Prince Mohammed Bin Abdulaziz International Airport
  • After your immigration at Prince Mohammed Bin Abdulaziz International Airport, you'll be transported to your reserved Hotel in the Holy Madinah.
  • Meet with a Hijaz team member to fix the Umrah schedule and address any specific requests.
  • Perform Umrah under the guidance of our expert Umrah guide (Maolana) following Sharia guidance.
  • Optionally, download the Tawakkalna App to book your rawda visit in Madinah.
